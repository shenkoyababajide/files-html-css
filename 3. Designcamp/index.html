BENEFITS OF CODING

1. Problem-Solving Skill

Leaning to code teaches you to task your brain, analyse and solve different challenges. This skill, once acquired, surely transcend beyond your coding life, it does go on to shape you into a logically thinking being and an astute problem-solver.


2. Enhanced Self-Esteem

Learning and practicing coding gives you sense of accomplishment when you write and execute simple (beginner) and complex (intermediate) lines of code. You feel like you are a being with superpowers. The feeling that you can write code to create a dynamic website with full control under your keyboard is amazing and inspiring. Your confidence level increases day-by-day as you code.


3. Great Earning Potential

Although this should not be the driving force for learning to code, but the salary of an average coder always call for attention. The annual salary of coding and programming-related professionals according to Bureau of Labour Statistics was put at $70,000 - $107,510. This is about 50% higher than average national wage for other professions.


4. High Job Demand

Odd right? Highly-paying job with extremely high job demand. This is the reality of coding-related jobs. Every year, opportunity for coders increase by double digits. Many job opportunities have combined programing skills into numerous job descriptions, which has shot its demand higher and higher every year.
